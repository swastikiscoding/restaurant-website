About Us

Our Story

Tandoori Tadka was born from a passion for authentic Indian cuisine, bringing the rich and diverse flavors of India to your plate. Since our inception, we have focused on crafting dishes that are not only delicious but also steeped in tradition. Whether it's a family gathering or a special event, we aim to create memorable experiences for all our guests.

With a commitment to quality ingredients and time-honored recipes, our chefs blend spices and flavors to bring out the best in every dish. From starters to desserts, each offering tells a story of India's culinary heritage.

Meet the Chefs

Chef Akshat Jha

Chef Akshat Jha specializes in traditional North Indian cuisine, mastering the art of the tandoor. His dedication to authenticity and attention to detail ensures that every dish carries the rich flavors of India's northern regions.

Chef Aarushi Vashisht

Chef Aarushi Vashisht brings a modern twist to Indian cuisine, blending traditional methods with contemporary flavors. Her innovative approach to food has won the hearts of many, creating a unique dining experience at Tandoori Tadka.

Chef Vivek Aggarwal

Chef Vivek Aggarwal is the master of spices, crafting flavorful curries and dishes that take you on a culinary journey across India. His expertise lies in balancing the spices to bring out the true essence of every ingredient.
